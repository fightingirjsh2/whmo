The announcement that Destiny 2 will receive its final live service content update on June 9th, 2026 hit thousands of fans of Bungie's first person shooter hard on Thursday. In the aftershock of the announcement, communities around the internet, but particularly on Twitter (X, bleh) we're ablaze with nostalgic memories of times passed, clips that only a Destiny fan could appreciate, and zealous debate about the past and future for the series we all love (and sometimes hate, but in a loving way)., ranging from Marathon fans dunking on Destiny fans, crackpot ideas on how to save the series, and postmortems on what went wrong over the past 5-6 years, and more narrowly in the last 12-18 months. Amongst all of these discussions, it was certaintly clear that most of these people want more Destiny, with various hashtags circulating in discussions, #WewantDestiny3 #SaveDestiny and more.

I plan on writing about each of those discussions in seperate articles here, but in this piece I want to discuss; if we are going to put our collective energy into begging for one thing, should it be Destiny 3, or a revival of Destiny 2?
